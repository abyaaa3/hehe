An international engineering design competition where university teams conceive, design, build & race a formula-style car — judged on engineering excellence, not just speed.

Phase 1
Weeks 1–4
LEARN 📚
No one touches CAD before understanding the rules, theory, and tools. Foundation weeks — the most important phase.
✓ Full software stack installed
✓ Rulebook read & compliance mapped
✓ Concept Brief per subsystem
✓ Benchmarked top 3 global teams
Phase 2
Weeks 5–8
DESIGN 🔧
Think before you make. Generate concepts, validate analytically, present PDR. No physical building yet — design on paper first.
✓ 2–3 design concepts per subsystem
✓ FEA + CFD simulations run
✓ Preliminary Design Review (PDR)
✓ Sponsor deck sent to 10+ companies
Phase 3
Weeks 9–12
COMMIT 🏁
Design is approved. Build it in full CAD, validate every component, integrate subsystems, freeze the design. What is designed is what gets manufactured.
✓ Frozen design — zero placeholder geometry
✓ FEA convergence studies complete
✓ Design report draft submitted
✓ 2+ confirmed sponsors

🧠 Skills by Subsystem
click to expand
🏗
Chassis & Frame
SolidWorks · ANSYS · TIG Welding
▼
Software: SolidWorks, ANSYS Structural, Altair HyperMesh  ·  Learn: Space frame vs monocoque, torsional stiffness FEA, SAE tube diameter rules (Sec T)  ·  Book: Carroll Smith "Engineer to Win"
🔩
Suspension & Steering
Kinematics · Geometry · Dynamics
▼
Software: OptimumKinematics, Lotus Shark, ADAMS Car, MATLAB  ·  Learn: Double wishbone geometry, camber gain, Ackermann steering, roll centres  ·  Book: Milliken "Race Car Vehicle Dynamics"
⚙️
Powertrain & Drivetrain
Engine · Gearbox · LSD
▼
Software: GT-POWER, Ricardo WAVE, MATLAB/Simulink  ·  Learn: Gear ratios, torque curves, LSD vs open diff, restricted intake rules  ·  Book: A. Graham Bell "Four-Stroke Performance Tuning"
💨
Aerodynamics
CFD · Downforce · Drag
▼
Software: ANSYS Fluent, OpenFOAM, Star-CCM+, CATIA surface modelling  ·  Learn: Front wing profiles, undertray diffusers, Cl/Cd ratios, downforce vs drag trade-off  ·  Book: Joseph Katz "Race Car Aerodynamics"
⚡
Electronics & EV Systems
ECU · CAN Bus · BMS · Data
▼
Software: KiCad, Altium Designer, MATLAB/Simulink, Arduino IDE, MoTeC i2  ·  Learn: CAN bus protocol, BMS cell balancing, IMU + data acquisition  ·  Ref: TI CAN tutorials, FS Electric rules supplement
📊
Reports & Business
Design report · Cost · Business plan
▼
Software: LaTeX / MS Word, Excel BOM, PowerPoint pitch deck  ·  Learn: DFMEA, design decision matrices, NPV business case, cost-per-part breakdown  ·  Ref: FSG Design report samples, SAE cost report templates
